# Show Information: Mission, Contacts, and Meeting Logistics

**Quick answer:** The Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo is at 3 NRG Park, Houston, Texas 77054. The main line is 832.667.1000. The Membership Office (832.667.1073) handles member questions, and the Main, Membership, and Ticket Offices are open Monday to Friday, 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. A full department phone directory and the 2026 office closures are below.

## What this is

The reference material a committee member needs about the organization itself: who HLSR is, how to reach each department, when the offices are open, and where the Show is located. It comes from the Show Information section that opens the 2026 Volunteer Handbook. This doc is the canonical source for the Show's mission, addresses, phone numbers, and hours. For membership, dues, and badging, see the membership doc (`handbook-volunteer-2026-membership-volunteer-information`).

## Mission statement

The Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo promotes agriculture by providing a family-friendly experience that educates and entertains the public, supports Texas youth, showcases Western heritage, and delivers year-round educational support within the community.

## Address

Both the physical and the mailing address are the same:

Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo, NRG Center, 3 NRG Park, Houston, Texas 77054.

## Lobby and office hours

The Main Office, the Membership Office, and the Ticket Office are all open Monday to Friday, 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. Lobby hours are subject to change, so check the website for the latest times.

## Emergencies during the Show

If there is an emergency during the Show, follow the instructions of venue staff and official announcements over the NRG Park PA systems, and seek shelter or evacuate as directed.

## FAQ

**What is the Show's main phone number and address?**
The main line is 832.667.1000. The address (both physical and mailing) is Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo, NRG Center, 3 NRG Park, Houston, Texas 77054.

**Who do I call with a membership or badge question?**
The Membership Office at 832.667.1073.

**Who handles donations made through the Souvenir Program?**
Donor Management at 832.667.1124.

**What are the office hours, and what holidays are the offices closed?**
The Main Office, Membership Office, and Ticket Office are open Monday to Friday, 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. (subject to change). In 2026 the offices are closed for Juneteenth (Jun. 19), Independence Day (July 3), Labor Day (Sept. 7), Thanksgiving (Nov. 26 to 27), Christmas (Dec. 23 to 25), and New Year's Eve (Dec. 31).
