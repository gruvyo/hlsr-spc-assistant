# SPC leadership and chain of command (2027 Show Year)

**Quick answer:** The Souvenir Program Committee is led by Chairman Trey Cranson. Below him are four Vice Chairman divisions: Administration (Vice Chairman Jessica Smith), Communication (Vice Chairman Joe Torres), Recruitment (Vice Chairman Rosemary Simons), and Sales (Vice Chairmen Ron Kotlar, Adam Rice, and Carol Kohanski). Sales is organized into ten teams, each with a captain and assistant captains. Use this directory to find who leads your division or team.

## What this is

The chain of command for the Souvenir Program Committee for the 2027 Show Year. It names the Chairman, the four Vice Chairman divisions, and the captains, assistant captains, and coordinators within each. It also lists the ten sales teams with their captains and assistant captains. If you want to know who leads your area, who your captain is, or who sits above your team, this is the page. Entries marked TBD are positions that had not been filled at the time this directory was prepared.

## FAQ

**Who is the SPC Chairman?**
Trey Cranson is the Chairman of the Souvenir Program Committee for the 2027 Show Year.

**What are the divisions on the committee?**
There are four Vice Chairman divisions: Administration (Jessica Smith), Communication (Joe Torres), Recruitment (Rosemary Simons), and Sales (Ron Kotlar, Adam Rice, and Carol Kohanski).

**Who leads Sales?**
Sales has three Vice Chairmen: Ron Kotlar, Adam Rice, and Carol Kohanski. It is organized into ten teams, each with its own captain.

**Who is my team captain?**
Find your team number in the Sales table above. For example, Team 1 is led by Cindy Trahan, Team 2 by Jené Raison, Team 4 by Sandra Torres, and Team 10 by Jon Stancik. Team 9's captain was TBD at the time this directory was prepared.

**Who handles Frames?**
Frames sits under the Administration division, led by Captain Joy Maddox.

**Who handles Hospitality?**
Hospitality is in the Administration division, led by Captain Callie Brown, with Assistant Captain Peter Young.

**Who are the Outreach coordinators?**
External Communications (Outreach), in the Communication division, has four coordinators: Mark Bender, Stephanie Proffitt, Meredith Crockett, and Ryan Murphy.

**Who leads Recruitment?**
Vice Chairman Rosemary Simons leads the Recruitment division, with Captain Sabrina Martinez and Assistant Captains John Simons and Andrea Browne.

**What does TBD mean in this directory?**
TBD marks a position that had not been filled at the time this directory was prepared.
