# HLSR W-9 (Request for Taxpayer Identification Number and Certification)

**Quick answer:** This is the completed IRS Form W-9 for the Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo, Inc. Its legal name is Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo, Inc, its address is 3 NRG Park, Houston, TX 77054, and its taxpayer ID (EIN) is 74-1142851. Give it to an advertiser or donor whose company needs HLSR's tax information to set the Show up as a vendor or to issue a payment.

## What this is

A W-9 is the IRS form a business hands out so another business can record its correct taxpayer identification number (TIN) and legal name. When you ask a company to buy an ad or make a donation, their accounts payable or vendor-setup team often asks for the payee's W-9 before they can cut a check. This is the Show's pre-filled, signed copy. You can download it from the dashboard and send it to the company so the sale or donation isn't held up.

It's a standard IRS form (Form W-9, Rev. March 2024). The Show has already filled it in and signed it, so you don't need to complete anything. You just forward it.

## What the form says

The completed fields on the Show's W-9:

- **Line 1 (Name of the entity):** Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo, Inc
- **Line 2 (Business name or disregarded entity name, if different):** blank
- **Line 3a (Federal tax classification):** C corporation (this box is checked)
- **Line 3b (foreign partners/owners/beneficiaries):** not checked
- **Line 4 (Exemptions):** the Exempt payee code (if any) field is filled in as 5. The Exemption from FATCA reporting code field is blank. Exempt payee code 5 on a W-9 identifies a corporation, meaning the Show is an exempt payee for backup withholding.
- **Line 5 (Address):** 3 NRG Park
- **Line 6 (City, state, ZIP):** Houston, TX 77054
- **Line 7 (Account numbers):** blank
- **Requester's name and address:** blank
- **Part I, Taxpayer Identification Number (TIN):** the Employer Identification Number (EIN) box is filled in as 74-1142851. The Social Security number box is left blank, which is correct for a business.
- **Part II, Certification:** signed by hand. There's a handwritten signature on the signature line, "AP SUPERVISOR" is written in the space next to it, and the date is 1/14/2025.

## FAQ

**My advertiser's accounts payable says they need a W-9 before they can pay. Where do I get it?**
It's on the SPC Dashboard. Open the Links widget, go to the Forms, handbooks & maps section at the bottom, and click HLSR W9 Form. Download the PDF and send it to their AP contact.

**What is the Show's EIN (tax ID number)?**
74-1142851. It's in the Employer Identification Number box in Part I of the W-9.

**What name and address should I give for the Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo?**
The legal name is Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo, Inc, and the address is 3 NRG Park, Houston, TX 77054.

**Do I need to fill anything in or sign it?**
No. The Show's copy is already completed and signed. Just send the PDF as-is.

**Is the Rodeo a corporation or a nonprofit on this form?**
On line 3a the federal tax classification box checked is C corporation. That's what the W-9 reports, so that's what you should pass along if asked. The form also enters an Exempt payee code of 5 on line 4, which on a W-9 means a corporation and tells the payer the Show is exempt from backup withholding.

**The company wants a Social Security number on the form. Is that missing?**
No. For a business you use the Employer Identification Number, not a Social Security number, so the SSN box is correctly left blank and the EIN (74-1142851) is filled in instead.
