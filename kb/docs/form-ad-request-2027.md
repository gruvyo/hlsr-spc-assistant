# Advertiser Information Form (2027)

**Quick answer:** This is an information form, not an order form. You give it to your advertiser to collect their company details and ad selection. It is not sent to HLSR. The advertiser returns it to you, the sales member, and you enter the official contract through the SPC online ordering system. Ad page options run from a Half Page Black/White at $500 up to a Reserved Two Pages at $3.5K. New artwork is due December 4, 2026. Payment is Net 30.

## What this is

The Advertiser Information Form is the intake sheet a Souvenir Program Committeeman hands to an advertiser. It gathers the advertiser's contact and billing details plus their ad choice so the member has what they need to place the order. Two warnings are printed right at the top: "NOT AN ORDER FORM" and "Do not send to HLSR - Return to the Member." The form's revision tag is Form 04.27.26.

The form does not create the contract. After the advertiser fills it out and returns it to the member, the member enters the official contract through the SPC online ordering system. The advertiser then gets an email to authorize and pay.

## Advertiser information collected

The form asks the advertiser to provide:

- Industry
- Ad Name (as it will appear in the Program Index)
- Company Name
- Email
- Contact First Name
- Contact Last Name
- Billing Address
- City, State, Zip
- Phone

## Advertisement page options and prices

Select one. Prices are in USD.

| Option | Price |
|---|---|
| One Page Color | 1,000 |
| Two Page Color | 2,000 |
| Half Page Color | 750 |
| Entertainer Half Page | 1,000 |
| Entertainer Full Page | 2,000 |
| Half Page Black/White | 500 |
| Reserved One Page | 2,000 |
| Reserved Two Pages | 3,500 |

A Reserved Page means placement of the ad as it ran the previous year, or placement to a specific page number or area of the book if available. There's a line on the form to indicate the requested page numbers.

## Artwork: new versus repeat

The form has two artwork paths:

- New artwork. The advertiser submits new artwork with a deadline of December 4, 2026.
- Repeat. Repeat the ad from the previous year. There's a blank to write the page number the ad ran on previously ("REPEAT the Ad from Previous Year on Page # ___").

## Payment

Payment is due Net 30 days.

The advertiser can contact HLSR Accounting any time to pay by card at 832.667.1099. They will also receive an invoice and a Pay Now link within 10 days, once the member places the order.

## Important: send to the sales member

The form repeats the process so nobody mails it to HLSR by mistake:

- This form is only to gather information from the advertiser.
- The Souvenir Program Committeeman who provided the form must enter the official contract through the SPC online ordering system.
- Do not mail the form to HLSR. Return it to the sales member.
- Once the member enters the contract, the advertiser receives an email marked ACTION NEEDED. That email lets them authorize and pay for the order, and it contains a link to the Artwork Guidelines.
- The advertiser should receive an invoice and payment link within 10 days after the member places the order.

## FAQ

**Do I send this form to HLSR?**
No. It says "Do not send to HLSR" right at the top. It's an information form, not an order. The advertiser returns it to you, the sales member, and you enter the official contract through the SPC online ordering system.

**How much does each ad size cost?**
One Page Color is $1K, Two Page Color is $2K, Half Page Color is $750, Entertainer Half Page is $1K, Entertainer Full Page is $2K, Half Page Black/White is $500, Reserved One Page is $2K, and Reserved Two Pages is $3.5K.

**What is a Reserved Page?**
It's placement of the ad as it ran the previous year, or placement to a specific page number or area of the book if that's available. Write the requested page numbers on the form. Reserved One Page is $2K and Reserved Two Pages is $3.5K.

**When is artwork due?**
New artwork is due December 4, 2026. If the advertiser wants to repeat last year's ad instead, write the page number it ran on previously in the "REPEAT the Ad from Previous Year" line.

**When does the advertiser pay, and how?**
Payment is Net 30. They can pay by card any time by calling HLSR Accounting at 832.667.1099, or wait for the invoice and Pay Now link, which arrives within 10 days after the member places the order.

**What happens after I enter the contract?**
The advertiser gets an email marked ACTION NEEDED. It lets them authorize and pay for the order and includes a link to the Artwork Guidelines. The invoice and payment link follow within 10 days.
