# SPC meeting schedule and events (2027 Show Year)

**Quick answer:** The committee has six events on the calendar for the 2027 Show Year, held in 2026: the Annual Crawfish Boil on May 17, 2026 at 2:00 PM on the Moonshine Deck, and five General Meetings on June 11, July 14, August 11, September 8, and October 13, 2026. General Meeting 1 is offsite at the Houston Saengerbund at 6:00 PM. The other four are at NRG Center at 6:15 PM. RSVP through the Event Schedule widget on the SPC Dashboard.

## What this is

The Souvenir Program Committee's schedule of meetings and events for the 2027 Show Year. The Show Year runs ahead of the season, so these meetings are held in 2026 even though they belong to the 2027 Show Year. Use this to know when and where each meeting is, and to RSVP.

The season opens with the Annual Crawfish Boil, a social event, and then runs through five General Meetings from June to October. Most General Meetings are at NRG Center. The first General Meeting is offsite at the Houston Saengerbund.

To RSVP, use the Event Schedule widget on the SPC Dashboard at https://dashboard.rodeospc.com. RSVPs open one week before each event, so if you do not see the RSVP link yet, check back closer to the meeting. The schedule can change, so confirm it on the dashboard before an event.
