# How to Save a Hi-Res, Print-Ready PDF in Adobe Illustrator

**Quick answer:** Open your ad in Illustrator, go to File then Save As, name the file, change the file type to Adobe PDF, and click Save. In the Save Adobe PDF window, change the Preset to **[High Quality Print]**, check that no boxes are checked under Marks and Bleeds, then click Save PDF. Your new hi-res PDF lands in the same folder as the original file.

## What this is

A short how-to for turning your finished Illustrator ad into a high resolution PDF, also called a High Quality Print file. This is the print-ready format HLSR needs for the Souvenir Program. Open your Adobe Illustrator (.ai) file first, then follow the steps below.

## Step 1: File, then Save As

Go to **File > Save As**.

A window may open asking whether you want to save on your computer or to the cloud. Choose to save **on your computer**.

## Step 2: Name the file and set the type to Adobe PDF

In the Save As window:

- **Enter the file name.** Use the format `Contract#_Company_Name`.
- **Change the file type.** Open the "Save as type" dropdown and choose **Adobe PDF (\*.PDF)**.
- Click **Save**.

Tip: Save it into the same folder as your original Illustrator file so the two stay together and the PDF is easy to find.

## Step 3: Set the PDF options

A new window opens, the Save Adobe PDF dialog.

- **Change the Preset to [High Quality Print].** Open the "Adobe PDF Preset" dropdown at the top and select **[High Quality Print]**. This is what makes the PDF high resolution.
- **Check Marks and Bleeds.** Click "Marks and Bleeds" in the list on the left side of the window and make sure **no boxes are checked** there.
- Click **Save PDF**.

## Step 4: You're done

That's it. Close your file. Your new hi-res PDF is saved in the same folder as the original Illustrator file, ready to submit through the artwork form.

## FAQ

**What preset do I pick for a print-ready PDF?**
[High Quality Print]. It's in the Adobe PDF Preset dropdown at the top of the Save Adobe PDF window. That preset is what gives you the high resolution file HLSR needs.

**What does "make sure no boxes are checked" under Marks and Bleeds mean?**
After you pick the preset, click "Marks and Bleeds" in the list on the left of the Save Adobe PDF window. Leave every checkbox there unchecked, so no crop marks, trim marks, or other printer marks get added to your file.

**Where does my PDF go after I save it?**
It saves to the folder you chose in the Save As window. If you saved it next to your original .ai file, both will be in the same folder.

**What should I name the file?**
Use `Contract#_Company_Name`, for example your contract number, then an underscore, then the company name.

**Do I save to my computer or to the cloud?**
Save on your computer. If Illustrator asks where to save, pick the on-your-computer option, not the cloud.

**My ad is in InDesign, not Illustrator. Is this the same?**
The idea is the same but the steps differ. See the InDesign version of this how-to instead.
