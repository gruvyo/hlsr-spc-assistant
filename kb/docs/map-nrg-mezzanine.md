# NRG Center Mezzanine level map

**Quick answer:** This is the floor map for the NRG Center Mezzanine (the second floor), where many SPC meetings and events are held. The meeting rooms are grouped into four numbered blocks (the 200s, 300s, 400s, and 500s/600s). If your event location is just a room number like 604-605, it's on this floor. Café on the Park, the lobby, and the rotunda are also up here.

## What this is

A wayfinding map of the NRG Center Mezzanine level, also called the NRG Center 2nd Floor. It shows the meeting room layout, the room numbers, Café on the Park, the lobby and rotunda, and the location of restrooms, elevators, escalators, stairs, and freight elevators. The map orients with a north arrow in the top right corner.

The meeting rooms come in two types, shown in two shades on the map. One set are standard meeting rooms with a ceiling height of 18 feet. The other set are meeting rooms with windows that overlook the Exhibit Hall.

## Meeting room blocks and numbers

The rooms are arranged in four numbered blocks. Within each row the numbers count up from right (east) to left (west), so reading the map left to right the numbers run high to low. Here is each row in actual left-to-right (west to east) map order:

**500 and 600 block (far left).** Top row 504, 503, 502, 501. Two rows below it: 611, 610, 609, 608, 607, 606 across the top and 605, 604, 603, 602, 601, 600 below, plus room 500 standing alone to the right of that group.

**400 and 300 block (center).** The 400 rooms run 409, 408, 407, 406, 405, 404, 403 across the top, with room 410 standing alone at the far left of the block. The 300 rooms are in two rows: 313, 312, 311, 310, 309, 308, 307 across the top and 306, 305, 304, 303, 302, 301, 300 below.

**200 block (right, east end).** Room 400 stands alone in the upper area above the west end of the 200 rooms. The 200 rooms run 207, 206, 205, 204, 203, 202, 201 in a row, with room 208 standing alone to the left (west) of 207 and room 200 standing alone to the right (east) of 201.

If you only have a bare room number for an event (for example 604-605), it's in NRG Center on this Mezzanine level. Use this map to find it.

## Café on the Park, lobby, and rotunda

**Café on the Park** is on the left (west) side of the floor, just off the lobby. It is 9,818 gross square feet (GSF).

**Lobby and rotunda.** The lobby runs along the front of the floor. The combined lobby and rotunda area is 7,500 GSF. The rotunda is the round area toward the center-right of the floor.

A 30-foot dimension is marked across the open central area between the lobby and the room blocks.

## Map legend

The map uses these symbols and colors:

- Column (gray dot)
- ATM (green dollar-sign marker)
- Meeting Rooms, ceiling height 18 feet (darker teal)
- Meeting Rooms with windows that overlook the Exhibit Hall (lighter teal)
- Stairs
- Escalator
- Freight Elevator (purple FE marker)
- Elevator
- Restrooms (yellow marker). All restrooms are ADA accessible.
- Family Restroom (purple marker). All restrooms are ADA accessible.
- Café on the Park (purple block)

EXIT markers are placed around the perimeter of the floor.

## FAQ

**My event location is just a room number like 604-605. Where is that?**
It's on the NRG Center Mezzanine level (the second floor). A bare numbered room means NRG Center, and this map shows where each numbered room is. Room 604 and 605 are in the 600 block on the far left (west) side of the floor.

**Which floor is this map for?**
The Mezzanine level of NRG Center, also called the 2nd floor. On the portal it's listed as the NRG Center 2nd Floor Map.

**Are the restrooms accessible?**
Yes. The legend states that all restrooms are ADA accessible, including the family restroom.

**What's the difference between the two meeting room colors?**
The darker teal rooms are standard meeting rooms with an 18-foot ceiling height. The lighter teal rooms are meeting rooms with windows that overlook the Exhibit Hall.

**How big is Café on the Park?**
9,818 gross square feet. It sits on the west side of the floor next to the lobby.

**Where are the elevators and escalators?**
The legend marks elevators, escalators, stairs, and freight elevators (the purple FE markers). They're spaced around the central area between the lobby and the room blocks. Look for the matching legend symbols on the map.
