# Souvenir Program donor benefit packages

**Quick answer:** Donors to the HLSR Education Fund through the SPC get benefits that scale with the donation level. The three published 2026 tiers are $2,000 to $9,999, $10,000 to $19,999, and $20,000+ (Scholarship Donor). Donations above $20,000 get custom benefits arranged with HLSR Donor Relations at 832.667.1124. To make a donation, see the how-to-give doc (`donor-how-to-give`).

## What this is

The benefit packages for donations to the HLSR Education Fund made through the SPC. Each tier lists the benefits and the fair market value (FMV) of each item, which matters because it affects how much of the donation is tax-deductible. The 2026 values below are the canonical, current figures. A superseded 2024 table is kept at the end for historical reference only. FMV figures are in USD.

For the mechanics of collecting a donation, the paper contract fields, the personalized online donation link, and the donor-site walkthrough, see the how-to-give doc (`donor-how-to-give`). For the Show-wide donor policy framework (donor categories, recognition and donor-board rules, badge access rights), see the donor program handbook section (`handbook-volunteer-2026-donor-program`).

## How a donation and its benefits work

Donors give to the Education Fund through the SPC website. After the donation is received, the donor gets an email asking them to accept or decline each benefit. The donor has to open that email and complete it, or the benefits can be delayed. A member entering a donation on a donor's behalf uses the Donation Contract in the SPC online system, then reminds the donor to respond to the benefits acknowledgment email.

**Tax note:** the portion of a contribution that may be tax-deductible is the amount given above the total fair market value of the goods and services the donor receives. Donors should consult a tax professional.

## FAQ

**What donation levels have published benefit packages?**
Three: $2,000 to $9,999, $10,000 to $19,999, and $20,000+ (Scholarship Donor). Above $20,000, benefits are arranged directly with HLSR Donor Relations.

**How much of my donation is tax-deductible?**
Generally the amount you give above the total fair market value of the benefits you receive. Check with your tax professional.

**I donated. Why haven't my benefits arrived?**
After your donation you get an email asking you to accept or decline each benefit. If you don't open and complete it, your benefits can be delayed.

**What does a member do to enter a donation?**
Enter the Donation Contract through the SPC online system, then remind the donor to respond to the benefits acknowledgment email. Full mechanics are in the how-to-give doc (`donor-how-to-give`).

### 2024 badge access footnotes (historical)

- **Silver badge (2024):** Each silver badge allowed access for two people to all 2024 Show events in NRG Center and NRG Arena. It did not provide NRG Stadium access or admission to Rodeo Uncorked! events.
- **Gold badge (2024):** Each gold badge allowed access for two people to all 2024 Show events throughout NRG Park (except Rodeo Uncorked! events), including the main concourse of NRG Stadium for RODEOHOUSTON performances. Gold badges did not guarantee a stadium seat or allow club or suite level access.
- The Scholarship Banquet invitation and recognition benefit is not available for 4-H and FFA scholarship donations.
