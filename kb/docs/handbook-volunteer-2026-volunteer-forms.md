# Volunteer Handbook: Volunteer forms

**Quick answer:** This page lists the volunteer forms available through the handbook, grouped into four categories. Accounting has the Committee Deposit Reconciliation Form and the Committeeman's Expense Reimbursement Form. Equipment has the 2026 Estimated Golf Cart Rates and the 2026 Equipment Rate Card. Legal has the Committee Entertainment Contract Request. Membership has the Pickup Authorization Form, three Release of Liability, Indemnity and Background Check Authorization Agreements (one for under 18, one for ages 18 to 20, and one general), and the Committee Roster Publication Form.

## What this is

A single index page from the 2026 Volunteer Handbook that gathers the volunteer forms in one place. Each form name is a clickable link in the handbook (shown with a "+" next to it) that opens the form. The forms are grouped under four headings: Accounting, Equipment, Legal, and Membership.

## FAQ

**What forms can I find in the handbook?**
The handbook groups them into four categories. Accounting has the Committee Deposit Reconciliation Form and the Committeeman's Expense Reimbursement Form. Equipment has the 2026 Estimated Golf Cart Rates and the 2026 Equipment Rate Card. Legal has the Committee Entertainment Contract Request. Membership has the Pickup Authorization Form, three Release of Liability, Indemnity and Background Check Authorization Agreements, and the Committee Roster Publication Form.

**Which form do I use to get reimbursed for an expense?**
The Committeeman's Expense Reimbursement Form, under the Accounting category.

**Which release form do I use for a minor?**
There are three Release of Liability, Indemnity and Background Check Authorization Agreements under Membership. Use the under 18 version for anyone under 18, the ages 18 to 20 version for someone 18 to 20, and the general version otherwise.

**Where do I find the equipment and golf cart pricing?**
Under the Equipment category. The 2026 Estimated Golf Cart Rates covers golf cart pricing and the 2026 Equipment Rate Card covers the rest.

**How do I open one of these forms?**
Each form name in the handbook is a link (marked with a "+"). Click the form name to open it.

**Which form publishes our committee roster?**
The Committee Roster Publication Form, under the Membership category.
