# Personalized donation link instructions

**Quick answer:** Sign in at www.rodeospc.com, scroll to the Shareable Donation Link widget, and click Copy. Paste that link into your email, text, or document. When a donor clicks it, the donation site opens with your name already selected as the Volunteer, so you get credit for the donation. The rest of this doc walks through each panel the donor fills out.

## What this is

The official instructions (Revision Basic, dated 2 July 2019) for finding your personalized donation contract entry link and understanding how the donation site works from the donor's side. The link is how a donor gives online while making sure the gift is credited to you. The same instructions also cover every step the donor goes through after they click your link.

## Where to find your personalized link and how to use it

1. In your browser, go to www.rodeospc.com.
2. Sign in.
3. If this is your first time on the site, click "First Time Loggin in" and follow the prompts.
4. Scroll down until you see the "Shareable Donation Link" widget.
5. Click the "Copy" button.
6. That action puts the link on your clipboard. Paste it into the document of your choice (email, text message, and so on).
7. When the donor clicks the link, it opens the donation website with your name already selected as the Volunteer, so you get credit for the donation.

**Rookie note:** If you are a Rookie and have not been added to the committee roster yet, you will not be able to log in to the rodeospc.com site. Contact your Team Captain with any questions or concerns.

## How the personalized link is created

The link has two parts, the base link and your member number (called the sales code).

- The non-member-specific link is `https://secure.rodeohouston.com/donate/?Code=SouvenirProgram`. The page opens with no member selected, so the customer can choose a member or not.
- Your personalized member-specific link is `https://secure.rodeohouston.com/donate/?Code=SouvenirProgram&salescode=XXXXXXXX`. The `XXXXXXXX` is your sales code, and the page opens with the right member (you) already selected.

The donation page is titled "Souvenir Program Educational Fund Contribution" (the screenshot in the source instructions shows it as "2020 Souvenir Program Educational Fund Contribution", with the show year in front). A line under the title reads "For questions regarding your online donation, please contact souvenirprogram@hlsr.com". The page asks "Did a Souvenir Program Volunteer refer you to make a donation?" with a YES / NO selection, followed by a "Volunteer Name" dropdown where the member is selected (your link pre-selects you).

## The donation panel

The donation panel shows the items set up to be purchased on the site. The customer enters the amount they wish to donate and clicks Continue.

## Entering customer information

After the amount, the customer enters their personal information. The form adapts to who they are:

- The customer is not an existing customer and not a company.
- The customer is a member and a company and does not know their customer number.
- The customer is a member and knows their customer number (they enter it directly).

If the information entered matches more than one customer record, the site shows a message that multiple customer records were found and asks the customer to select the correct one from a list (the list shows customer number, name, and email for each match).

## Benefit acceptance and rejection

The Benefit Acceptance and Rejection panel only appears if the donation amount entitles the donor to benefits.

The panel lists each benefit in a table with these columns: Benefit, Qty, FMV Per Unit, Accept, Reject, and Benefit Value Accepted. The donor accepts or rejects each one. (For example, recognition in the Official RODEOHOUSTON Souvenir Program carries a Dec. 15 deadline.)

The panel can also show optional instructional content, which displays in orange font, depending on how the site is configured.

## Additional information panel

For the Souvenir Program, the donor fills in two required fields:

- "I wish to remain anonymous" (required, YES / NO selection).
- "Listing in Souvenir Program" (required, free-form text, 25-character limit). The on-screen helper text notes this applies if your donation is 300 dollars or more, to enter N/A if not applicable, and that the space is limited to 25 characters including letters, punctuation, and spaces, with no phone numbers.

## Payment panel

The Payment panel is always shown and at a minimum allows payment by credit card (with Payment Type, Card Type, Card Number, Name On Card, and Expiration fields). Depending on how the site is configured, other payment options can appear:

- To Be Invoiced (Directors): a Director must provide their customer number as validation.
- To Be Invoiced (limited by dollar amount): if the order exceeds a set dollar amount, the To Be Invoiced option becomes available.
- Send in Payment: this option always requires a customer to be entered for the order.
- eCheck: if the site allows electronic check payments, this can be turned on. It always requires a customer to be entered for the order.

If Invoice, Send in Payment, and eCheck are all allowed, the Payment Type dropdown shows more options.

## Email confirmation panel

The Email Confirmation panel is always shown. It displays the donor's email address by default if a customer is selected. That address can be overwritten with a different one if needed. Whatever email address is entered here will receive the emailed confirmation.

## Confirmation page and emailed confirmation

After the order is submitted, a confirmation page appears showing the order details for all orders.

The emailed confirmation includes the same content as the confirmation page. It goes to the email address entered in the Email Confirmation panel. If a salesperson was selected for the donation, that salesperson is copied on the emailed confirmation, so they know a donation was made.

## FAQ

**How do I get my personal donation link?**
Sign in at www.rodeospc.com, scroll to the Shareable Donation Link widget, and click Copy. The link is now on your clipboard. Paste it wherever you want (email, text, a document).

**How does the link make sure I get credit for the donation?**
Your link carries your sales code (`&salescode=XXXXXXXX`). When the donor clicks it, the donation site opens with your name already selected as the Volunteer, so the gift is credited to you. If a salesperson was selected, you are also copied on the donor's emailed confirmation.

**I am a Rookie and cannot log in. Why?**
If you have not been added to the committee roster yet, you cannot log in to rodeospc.com. Contact your Team Captain.

**What does the donor have to fill out?**
The donation amount, their customer or company information, accept or reject any benefits they qualify for, the additional info fields (anonymous YES/NO and a 25-character Souvenir Program listing), payment, and a confirmation email address.

**What is the "Listing in Souvenir Program" field?**
It is a required free-form field, limited to 25 characters including letters, punctuation, and spaces, for donations of 300 dollars or more. It is how the donor's name appears in the program. Enter N/A if it does not apply. No phone numbers.

**What payment methods can a donor use?**
Credit card is always available. Depending on the site setup, the donor may also see To Be Invoiced (for Directors or for orders over a set dollar amount), Send in Payment, or eCheck. Send in Payment and eCheck always require a customer to be entered.
