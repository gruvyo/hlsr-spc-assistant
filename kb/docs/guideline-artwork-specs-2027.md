# 2027 Souvenir Program: Artwork Guidelines and Specifications

**Quick answer:** Ads must be print-ready CMYK PDFs, max 30 MB, submitted through the online artwork form by **Friday, December 4, 2026**. No proofs are provided. Files that aren't print-ready may incur fees and won't extend the deadline.

## Deadline and submission

The artwork deadline is Friday, December 4, 2026. Acceptable ads meet the specs below and are uploaded through the submission form link. Leave a phone number and email on the form so HLSR can reach you if there's a concern with the artwork. HLSR does not provide proofs.

## Trademark rules (important)

Use of HLSR marks is exclusive to Official Show Sponsors. Advertisers may not call themselves "sponsors" or "partners" of HLSR, and may not use HLSR logos or marks (including RODEOHOUSTON and specific committee names). "Supporter of Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo" is acceptable, and the trademark symbol is required.

## Standard ad sizes

All measurements are Width x Height.

| Ad unit | Size | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Full page (with bleed) | 8.625" x 11.125" | |
| Two-page ad | 8.625" x 11.125" each | Submit 2 separate files |
| Two-page spread | 17" x 11.125" overall | Submit as two full-page bleed ads. Keep the center 1" clear for binding. |
| Half page (horizontal only) | 7" x 4.75" | |

## Mechanical requirements

- File format: print-ready PDF, high resolution or high-quality print, max 30 MB.
- Live space: 7.375" x 9.875". Keep logos and important information inside this area.
- Bleed: keep all live content out of the 0.625" (5/8") strip at the bottom of the bleed page. That space is reserved for publication data.
- Trim size: 8.375" x 10.875".
- Color: CMYK only. No Pantones or RGB. Black should be 0,0,0,100.
- Images: 300 dpi, or 400 dpi if the image includes text, at final size in the layout.
- No crop marks: delete all crop marks before exporting.
- Printing: offset lithography (web) on 50 LB panaprint or equivalent.
- Binding: perfect binding. Full mechanical details available on request.
- Line screen: 150.

## Placement and renewal

The only way to request the same ad placement as the previous year is to buy the Reserved space. Entertainer ads can renew by completing a new order (authorized by the advertiser) by July 1, 2026. On July 2, unrenewed spaces become available.

## Rules and fees

- If artwork isn't print-ready and needs adjustments, fees may apply and the deadline doesn't move.
- Coupons that require a physical coupon are prohibited. Advertisers may ask consumers to "mention this ad" for an offer, but cannot require them to "show" the ad. Requiring a coupon may affect the Fair Market Value of the book.
- Missing artwork: if no artwork is submitted, the order is moved to a donation or cancelled and refunded.
- Ads in the Souvenir Program are not a charitable donation.
- HLSR does not provide proof of publication, and programs are not furnished to advertisers as part of their order.
- HLSR reserves the right to reject any advertisement and to omit orders without artwork or with delayed payment.

## Payment

Terms are Net 30 days. Every order gets an email with an invoice and a PAY NOW link within one month of the order, so keep the advertiser email current and check the spam folder. HLSR mails one physical invoice to the address on file. To pay by phone, call HLSR Accounting at 832.667.1099. If paying by company check, make sure the company name printed on the invoice is also on the check. Make checks payable to HLSR-Souvenir Program and mail to: HLSR-Souvenir Program, 3 NRG Park, Houston, Texas 77054.

## FAQ

**What's the artwork deadline for the 2027 program?**
Friday, December 4, 2026.

**What file format and size does the ad need to be?**
A print-ready PDF, high resolution, CMYK only, maximum 30 MB.

**Do I get a proof before it prints?**
No. HLSR does not provide proofs, and does not provide proof of publication after printing.

**What are the ad sizes?**
Full page (with bleed) 8.625" x 11.125", two-page ad (two files at the same size), two-page spread 17" x 11.125" overall, and half page (horizontal only) 7" x 4.75".

**Can I use the HLSR or RODEOHOUSTON logo in my ad?**
No. Those marks are for Official Show Sponsors only. You may say "Supporter of Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo" with the trademark symbol.

**How and when do I pay?**
Net 30 days. You'll get an emailed invoice with a PAY NOW link within a month, or pay by phone at 832.667.1099, or mail a check to HLSR-Souvenir Program, 3 NRG Park, Houston, Texas 77054.
