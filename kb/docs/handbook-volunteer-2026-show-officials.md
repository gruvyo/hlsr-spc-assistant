# Volunteer Handbook: Show Officials

**Quick answer:** The Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo is led by Chairman of the Board Pat Mann Phillips and President and CEO Chris Boleman, backed by a team of chief officers and legal counsel, an Executive Committee, and the Lifetime Members of the Executive Committee. For the complete list of the board of directors and lifetime directors, visit rodeohouston.com.

## What this is

The Show Officials section of the 2026 Volunteer Handbook. It introduces the people who run the Show, presented as a gallery of photo cards. The section opens with the two top leaders, moves through the chief officers and the two counsel who form the senior leadership team, and closes with two lists, the Executive Committee and the Lifetime Members of the Executive Committee. This page reproduces every name and title shown in that section.

## FAQ

**Who runs the Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo?**
The Chairman of the Board is Pat Mann Phillips and the President and CEO is Chris Boleman. They lead a team of chief officers, legal counsel, and the Executive Committee.

**Who is the Chairman of the Board, and who is the chairman-elect?**
Pat Mann Phillips is Chairman of the Board. Wesley Sinor is the chairman-elect of the board, listed second on the Executive Committee.

**Who handles legal matters at the Show?**
The Chief Legal Officer is Harry Perrin and the General Counsel is Chris Atmar. The handbook lists their titles in this section but does not give contact details here.

**Who is the Chief Volunteer officer?**
Mel Underhill, the Chief Volunteer and HR Officer.

**What is the difference between the Executive Committee and the Lifetime Members of the Executive Committee?**
The Executive Committee is the current governing group, headed by the chairman of the board and the chairman-elect. The Lifetime Members of the Executive Committee are people recognized with permanent standing on the committee. Both lists appear above.

**Where can I find the complete list of board members?**
This section is only a summary. For the full board of directors and lifetime directors, go to rodeohouston.com.
