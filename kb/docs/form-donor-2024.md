# 2024 Donor Form (Educational Fund Contribution Contract)

**Quick answer:** This is the paper form a committee member uses to collect a donor's information for a 2024 Educational Fund contribution. It records the donation amount, how the donor wants to be listed in the program, which education programs the money supports (for gifts of $20,000 or more), and which benefits the donor accepts or rejects at their giving level. The member collects the details here but must submit the actual order through the SPC online order form. No forms are accepted anywhere else.

## What this is

The 2024 Souvenir Program Committee Contract for Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo Educational Fund Contributions. It is a single-page contract used to collect a donor's commitment to the HLSR Educational Fund. The charity is the Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo Educational Fund, an Internal Revenue Code Section 501(c)(3) charitable organization. Its mailing address is P.O. Box 20070, Houston, Texas 77225-0070, and its Federal Tax Identification number is 76-0260204.

The form has five parts: a donation information block at the top, an educational program designation block, the donor benefits table, the donor information block, and the committee and payment information block at the bottom.

A note in the top right explains the tax treatment: the amount of the contribution that may be deductible for federal tax purposes is the excess value contributed by the donor over the value of goods or services received. Donors should consult a tax professional for more information.

## Donation information

The donor enters a **Donation Amount** in dollars.

There is a line for the exact listing the donor wants in the 2024 RODEOHOUSTON Souvenir Program. If it is left blank, recognition defaults to the Company or Individual Name listed elsewhere on the form. The listing rules are:

- A minimum of $10,000 is required for a listing in the program.
- Space is limited to 25 characters, including letters, punctuation, and spaces.
- No phone numbers.

## Educational program designation (gifts of $20,000 or more)

If the commitment is $20,000 or more and the donor wants to support specific programs, they designate how much goes to each program, with a minimum of $20,000 to at least one program. If total funding exceeds what a specific program needs, the Show reserves the right to shift all or part of the contribution to a different educational program.

The program checkboxes, each with a dollar amount line, are:

- Metropolitan
- Opportunity
- School Art
- 4-H / FFA
- However the Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo can best utilize my donation

There is also a **Special request** option. A donor can ask for something specific (for example a student from a specific school district, attending a specific college or university, or seeking a specific major) and write it on the special request line. Every effort is made to accommodate the request, but the Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo cannot guarantee special requests will be fulfilled.

## Donor benefits

Payment must be received before benefits can be distributed. The fair market value (FMV) is listed next to each benefit. The donor must reject or accept each applicable benefit by checking the Reject or Accept box, and write the accepted benefit's value in the Benefits Value Accepted column. No benefits are assumed or given unless checked Accepted.

### Badge and benefit footnotes

- **Silver badge:** Each silver badge allows access for two people to all 2024 Show events in NRG Center and NRG Arena. It does not provide NRG Stadium access or admission to Rodeo Uncorked! events. Tickets must be purchased to attend the rodeo or concert in NRG Stadium.
- **Gold badge:** Each gold badge allows access for two people to all 2024 Show events throughout NRG Park (except Rodeo Uncorked! events), including the main concourse of NRG Stadium for RODEOHOUSTON performances. Gold badges do not guarantee a seat in NRG Stadium or allow access to club or suite levels.
- The Scholarship Banquet invitation and recognition benefit is not available for 4-H and FFA scholarship donations.

## Donor information

The donor fills in their contact and signature details:

- Company / Individual Name
- Email Address (required for benefit notification and updates)
- Billing Address, Suite, City, State, ZIP
- Telephone Number (with area code)
- Contact Person (print), with Title / Position
- Authorized By (print), with Title / Position
- Authorized By (signature) and Date

The email address is required because the donor is notified about benefits there, and the donor must reject or accept each benefit for the package to be processed.

## How to submit

This form is to be used to collect information only. Members of the Souvenir Program Committee must submit all orders through the online order form. No forms will be accepted elsewhere.

## FAQ

**What is the minimum donation to get my name listed in the Souvenir Program?**
$10,000. The listing is limited to 25 characters (letters, punctuation, and spaces), with no phone numbers. If you leave the listing line blank, recognition defaults to the Company or Individual Name on the form. The listing deadline for the 2024 program was December 15, 2023.

**Do I have to do anything to get my benefits, or are they automatic?**
You have to choose them. The donor must reject or accept each applicable benefit, and no benefit is assumed or given unless it is checked Accepted. Payment also has to be received before any benefits can be distributed. A donor email address is required because benefit notifications and updates go there.

**How much of my donation is tax-deductible?**
The portion that may be deductible for federal tax purposes is the excess value you contribute over the fair market value of the goods or services you receive. Each benefit's FMV is listed on the form. Consult your tax professional for more information.

**I want to give $20,000 or more and direct it to a specific program. How does that work?**
On the form, designate how much goes to each program (Metropolitan, Opportunity, School Art, 4-H / FFA, or "however the Show can best utilize my donation"), with a minimum of $20,000 to at least one program. You can also write a special request, such as a student from a specific school district or college or seeking a specific major. The Show tries to accommodate requests but cannot guarantee them, and it may shift funding to a different program if one program is over-funded.

**What is the difference between a silver badge and a gold badge?**
A silver badge admits two people to all 2024 Show events in NRG Center and NRG Arena, but not NRG Stadium and not Rodeo Uncorked! events. A gold badge admits two people throughout NRG Park (except Rodeo Uncorked! events), including the main concourse of NRG Stadium for RODEOHOUSTON performances, but it does not guarantee a stadium seat or allow club or suite level access.

**Who do I contact about donating more than $20,000?**
The Development Office at 832.667.1074. They determine the benefits offered above the $20,000 Scholarship Donor level.

**As a committee member, where do I actually submit this?**
Through the SPC online order form. This paper form only collects the information. No forms are accepted anywhere else.
