# SPC New Western Silver Order Form (Buckles of Texas, 2026-27)

**Quick answer:** The Souvenir Program Committee partnered with Buckles of Texas to make a custom western silver line just for SPC members. You order by filling in the quantities on the form and emailing it straight to the vendor at bubba@murphybuckles.com (phone 832-595-7695). There are six items: cuff ($185 each), money clip ($99.50), pendant ($175), earrings ($90), badge ($125), and hat pick ($25). The sale is directly between you and the vendor, so they bill you (taxes and shipping included) and the committee is not responsible for the order.

## What this is

A one-page order form, with a second page of design proofs, for the SPC's custom western silver jewelry. The Souvenir Program Committee of HLSR enlisted Buckles of Texas to create specific items so members can buy them if they choose. To purchase, you fill out the order form with quantities and email it directly to Buckles of Texas. The transaction is between you and the vendor. The committee will not be responsible for any items ordered by committee members. The vendor contacts you directly about your order, and you receive an invoice that includes taxes and shipping.

**This is a member merchandise purchase, not an ad sale or a donation.** Buying silver is a member buying jewelry for themselves, vendor-direct. No money goes to HLSR, and it does not benefit the Show. Keep it separate from placing an ad (the SPC's ad-sales business) and from making a donation. To place an ad use the Ad Request Form; to give, use the donor process. This form is only for buying merchandise from the vendor.

The maker is A Cut Above Buckles by Andy Andrews (the proof page carries the "A Cut Above Buckles By Andy Andrews" logo with "Copyright 2023"). Buckles of Texas is the brand on the order form.

## How to order

1. Open the order form.
2. Fill in the quantity (qty) for each item you want.
3. Add your name in the NAME field, and fill in the DATE and SHIP TO fields at the top of the form.
4. Email the completed order form directly to Buckles of Texas at bubba@murphybuckles.com.
5. The vendor contacts you about your order and sends an invoice that includes taxes and shipping. You pay the vendor directly.

The form header is addressed "TO: Buckles of Texas, bubba@murphybuckles.com, 832-595-7695." The instruction line at the bottom of the order page repeats "Email your order to: bubba@murphybuckles.com."

## Items and prices

Prices are per each (EA). The form has a worked sample (1 badge ordered) that adds 8.25 percent sales tax, so a $125 badge comes to $135.31, with $10.31 tax and zero shipping in that example. Your actual invoice from the vendor will include taxes and shipping.

| Item | Description on form | Unit price (USD) |
|---|---|---|
| Cuff | Souvenir Program Committee item, CUFF | 185.00/EA |
| Money clip | DITTO, MONEY CLIP | 99.50/EA |
| Pendant | DITTO, PENDANT | 175.00/EA |
| Earrings | DITTO, EARINGS (spelled "EARINGS" on the form) | 90.00/EA |
| Badge | DITTO, BADGE | 125.00/EA |
| Hat pick | DITTO, HAT PICK | 25.00/EA |

"DITTO" on the form just means "same as above," another Souvenir Program Committee item. The form computes a subtotal, sales tax, shipping and handling, and total due as you enter quantities. The blank totals row on the form reads SUBTOTAL, SALES TAX, SHIPPING & HANDLING, TOTAL DUE.

## FAQ

**Is buying the silver the same as buying an ad or donating?**
No. Ordering silver is a member merchandise purchase, vendor-direct, a member buying jewelry for themselves. No money goes to HLSR and it does not benefit the Show. It is not an ad sale and not a donation. To place an ad use the Ad Request Form; to give, use the donor process.

**How do I order the silver?**
Fill in the quantities on the order form, add your name, date, and ship-to, then email the completed form to Buckles of Texas at bubba@murphybuckles.com. The vendor invoices you and ships directly.

**Who do I pay, the committee or the vendor?**
The vendor. This is a direct transaction between you and Buckles of Texas. The Souvenir Program Committee is not responsible for any items ordered. You get your invoice, with taxes and shipping, from the vendor.

**What can I buy and what does it cost?**
Six items: cuff ($185 each), money clip ($99.50), pendant ($175), earrings ($90), badge ($125), and hat pick ($25). Prices are per each.

**Are taxes and shipping included in those prices?**
No. The listed prices are the item unit prices. Your invoice from the vendor adds taxes and shipping. The sample on the form shows 8.25 percent sales tax (for example, a $125 badge plus $10.31 tax equals $135.31, with no shipping in that example).

**What's the contact for Buckles of Texas?**
Email bubba@murphybuckles.com or call 832-595-7695.

**What sizes are the pieces?**
The cuff is 5.50 inches wide, the money clip is 2.2 by 1.5 inches, the pendant is 2.3 by 2.8 inches, the earrings are 1.5 by 1.0 inches, the badge is 2.5 by 1.7 inches, and the hat pick is small (see design, with proof dimensions of 0.33, 0.37, and 0.75 inches). The proof note says it may not print to size but will be made as the size stated.
