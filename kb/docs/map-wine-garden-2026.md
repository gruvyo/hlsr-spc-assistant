# Champion Wine Garden map (2026)

**Quick answer:** This is the 2026 layout map of the Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo Champion Wine Garden, presented by Frost. It shows the location of every named bar, tent, stage, seating area, and restroom, plus the two entrances, the Seminar and Hospitality Gate, and which way NRG Center and NRG Stadium are. The Founders Tent is the spot for table reservations.

## What this is

A single-page graphical map of the Champion Wine Garden for the 2026 show. It uses a small icon legend (bar, tent, stage, restrooms) and labels each feature by name. It's a wayfinding aid, so use it to point someone toward a specific bar, tent, stage, or restroom, or to explain how the space is laid out. It does not list prices, hours, or menu items. For drinks and food, see the separate Wine Garden menu.

## Legend

The map marks four kinds of feature with icons:

- **Bar** (orange tent icon with a bottle)
- **Tent** (blue tent icon)
- **Stage** (guitar icon)
- **Restrooms** (a man-and-woman icon)

## Orientation and surroundings

The map has a compass rose, but it's rotated, not the usual north-up. On this map North points left, East points up, South points right, and West points down.

- **NRG Center** is off the left edge of the map, toward the top. The label runs vertically up the left side with an arrow pointing up toward it.
- **NRG Stadium** is off the bottom-right of the map. The label runs along the bottom edge with arrows pointing down toward it.

## Entrances and gates

- **Entrance** on the lower-left slanted edge of the garden, near the Tuscan Bar and the Tuscan Tent (toward the bottom-left corner).
- **Entrance** along the bottom of the garden, center, below the Wine Makers Corner and Tejas Bar.
- **Seminar and Hospitality Gate** at the bottom-right, on the NRG Stadium side near The Valley Tent and the restrooms.

## Bars

The orange bottle-tent icons mark the bars:

- **Vineyard Bar** (far left side of the garden, on the NRG Center end) - drawn as a column of three bar icons stacked together, so it's the largest bar on the map
- **Vineyard Hill Bar** (upper area, by the Vineyard Hill Seating)
- **Tuscan Bar** (left-center, below the Tuscan Tent)
- **Hillside Bar** (right-center, above the Hillside Seating)
- **Tejas Bar** (lower-center, by Wine Makers Corner)

## Tents

The blue tent icons mark the tents:

- **Founders Tent (Table Reservations)** (top-left, near the left entrance) - this is where table reservations are located
- **Bluebonnet Tent** (top, by the Vineyard Hill Seating)
- **Tuscan Tent** (left-center)
- **Hill Country Tent** (center, by the Hillside Seating)
- **High Plains Tent** (right side)
- **Fredericksburg Tent** (right side, below High Plains Tent)
- **Seminar Tent** (far right)
- **The Valley Tent** (bottom-right, near the Seminar and Hospitality Gate)
- **Wine Makers Corner** (lower-center, by the Tejas Bar)

## Stages

The guitar icons mark the stages:

- **Main Stage** (left-center of the garden, below the Vineyard Hill Bar)
- **Lone Star Stage** (center of the garden)

## Seating areas

- **Vineyard Hill Seating** (upper-left area, around the Bluebonnet Tent and Vineyard Hill Bar)
- **Hillside Seating** (center-right area, around the Hill Country Tent and Hillside Bar)

## Restrooms

There are restroom blocks marked with the man-and-woman icon in a few places:

- Near the top-left, by the Founders Tent (table reservations), along the left edge toward NRG Center (two stacked restroom blocks)
- Several restroom icons through the center of the garden, scattered between the Main Stage and the Lone Star Stage (the busiest restroom area on the map)
- At the bottom-right, near The Valley Tent and the Seminar and Hospitality Gate

## FAQ

**Where do I go to reserve a table?**
The Founders Tent. It's labeled "Founders Tent (Table Reservations)" and sits at the top-left of the garden, on the NRG Center side, by a pair of restroom blocks.

**Where are the restrooms?**
There are several. A pair near the top-left by the Founders Tent, a cluster of them through the center of the garden by the stages, and one at the bottom-right by The Valley Tent and the Seminar and Hospitality Gate.

**Which bars are in the Wine Garden?**
Five: Vineyard Bar, Vineyard Hill Bar, Tuscan Bar, Hillside Bar, and Tejas Bar.

**How do I get in?**
There are two entrances. One is on the lower-left slanted edge near the Tuscan Bar, and one is along the bottom of the garden in the center. There's also the Seminar and Hospitality Gate at the bottom-right on the NRG Stadium side.

**Which way is NRG Stadium versus NRG Center?**
NRG Center is off the left side of the map, toward the top, and NRG Stadium is off the bottom-right. Note the compass is rotated, so North points left, not up.

**Where are the stages?**
Two stages. The Main Stage is on the left-center side, and the Lone Star Stage is in the center of the garden.
