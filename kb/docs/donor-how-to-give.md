# How to make a donation (contract and online link)

**Quick answer:** A committee member collects a donor's information on the paper donation contract, then submits the actual order through the SPC online order form. To get credit, share your personalized donation link (sign in at www.rodeospc.com, copy the Shareable Donation Link widget) so your name is pre-selected as the Volunteer when the donor gives. Donations are due January 30, 2027 and billed by February 1, 2027. For the benefit values themselves, see the donor benefits doc (`donor-benefits`).

## What this is

The two ways to make a donation happen: the paper Educational Fund Contribution Contract that a member fills out with a donor, and the online donation site the donor (or member) actually submits through. This doc covers both. The paper form collects the information only. All orders are submitted through the SPC online order form, and no forms are accepted anywhere else.

For the donor benefit tiers and fair market values, see the donor benefits doc (`donor-benefits`) rather than the numbers here. For the Show-wide donor policy framework, see the donor program handbook section (`handbook-volunteer-2026-donor-program`).

## Deadline

Donations for the season are due **January 30, 2027** and are **billed by February 1, 2027**. (From the SPC chairman's June 2026 committee email.)

## The donation contract (paper form the member collects)

The Souvenir Program Committee Contract for HLSR Educational Fund Contributions is a single-page contract used to collect a donor's commitment to the Educational Fund. The charity is the Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo Educational Fund, an Internal Revenue Code Section 501(c)(3) charitable organization. Its mailing address is P.O. Box 20070, Houston, Texas 77225-0070, and its Federal Tax Identification number is 76-0260204.

A note on the form explains the tax treatment: the amount of the contribution that may be deductible for federal tax purposes is the excess value contributed by the donor over the value of goods or services received. Donors should consult a tax professional.

The form has five parts: a donation information block, an educational program designation block, the donor benefits table, the donor information block, and the committee and payment information block.

### Donation information

The donor enters a **Donation Amount** in dollars.

There is a line for the exact listing the donor wants in the RODEOHOUSTON Souvenir Program. If it is left blank, recognition defaults to the Company or Individual Name listed elsewhere on the form. The listing rules are:

- A minimum of $10,000 is required for a listing in the program.
- Space is limited to 25 characters, including letters, punctuation, and spaces.
- No phone numbers.

### Educational program designation (gifts of $20,000 or more)

If the commitment is $20,000 or more and the donor wants to support specific programs, they designate how much goes to each program, with a minimum of $20,000 to at least one program. If total funding exceeds what a specific program needs, the Show reserves the right to shift all or part of the contribution to a different educational program.

The program checkboxes, each with a dollar amount line, are:

- Metropolitan
- Opportunity
- School Art
- 4-H / FFA
- However the Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo can best utilize my donation

There is also a **Special request** option. A donor can ask for something specific (for example a student from a specific school district, attending a specific college or university, or seeking a specific major) and write it on the special request line. Every effort is made to accommodate the request, but the Show cannot guarantee special requests will be fulfilled.

### Donor benefits

Payment must be received before benefits can be distributed. The fair market value (FMV) is listed next to each benefit on the form. The donor must reject or accept each applicable benefit by checking the Reject or Accept box, and write the accepted benefit's value in the Benefits Value Accepted column. No benefits are assumed or given unless checked Accepted. The donor totals the accepted values in a **Total Benefits Amount Accepted** field at the bottom of the table.

For the benefit tiers and their fair market values, see the donor benefits doc (`donor-benefits`).

### Donor information

The donor fills in their contact and signature details:

- Company / Individual Name
- Email Address (required for benefit notification and updates)
- Billing Address, Suite, City, State, ZIP
- Telephone Number (with area code)
- Contact Person (print), with Title / Position
- Authorized By (print), with Title / Position
- Authorized By (signature) and Date

The email address is required because the donor is notified about benefits there, and the donor must reject or accept each benefit for the package to be processed.

### Committee and payment information

The committee member completes the bottom block:

**Committee information**

- Committee Member (print)
- Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo Member Number
- Committee Member Daytime Phone Number
- Team Number

**Payment information**

- Cash / Check option, with Amount Received and Check No.
- Send online Credit Card payment request option (the donor email address is required for this)

### How to submit the contract

This form is used to collect information only. Members of the Souvenir Program Committee must submit all orders through the online order form. No forms will be accepted elsewhere.

## The personalized online donation link

The personalized donation link is how a donor gives online while making sure the gift is credited to you.

### Find and share your link

1. In your browser, go to www.rodeospc.com.
2. Sign in.
3. If this is your first time on the site, click "First Time Loggin in" and follow the prompts.
4. Scroll down until you see the "Shareable Donation Link" widget.
5. Click the "Copy" button.
6. That action puts the link on your clipboard. Paste it into the document of your choice (email, text message, and so on).
7. When the donor clicks the link, it opens the donation website with your name already selected as the Volunteer, so you get credit for the donation.

**Rookie note:** If you are a Rookie and have not been added to the committee roster yet, you will not be able to log in to the rodeospc.com site. Contact your Team Captain with any questions or concerns.

### How the link is built

The link has two parts, the base link and your member number (called the sales code).

- The non-member-specific link is `https://secure.rodeohouston.com/donate/?Code=SouvenirProgram`. The page opens with no member selected, so the customer can choose a member or not.
- Your personalized member-specific link is `https://secure.rodeohouston.com/donate/?Code=SouvenirProgram&salescode=XXXXXXXX`. The `XXXXXXXX` is your sales code, and the page opens with the right member (you) already selected.

The donation page is titled "Souvenir Program Educational Fund Contribution" (with the show year in front, for example "2020 Souvenir Program Educational Fund Contribution"). A line under the title reads "For questions regarding your online donation, please contact souvenirprogram@hlsr.com". The page asks "Did a Souvenir Program Volunteer refer you to make a donation?" with a YES / NO selection, followed by a "Volunteer Name" dropdown where the member is selected (your link pre-selects you).

## The donor-site walkthrough

### Donation panel

The donation panel shows the items set up to be purchased on the site. The customer enters the amount they wish to donate and clicks Continue.

### Customer information

After the amount, the customer enters their personal information. The form adapts to who they are:

- The customer is not an existing customer and not a company.
- The customer is a member and a company and does not know their customer number.
- The customer is a member and knows their customer number (they enter it directly).

If the information entered matches more than one customer record, the site shows a message that multiple customer records were found and asks the customer to select the correct one from a list (the list shows customer number, name, and email for each match).

### Benefit acceptance and rejection

The Benefit Acceptance and Rejection panel only appears if the donation amount entitles the donor to benefits.

The panel lists each benefit in a table with these columns: Benefit, Qty, FMV Per Unit, Accept, Reject, and Benefit Value Accepted. The donor accepts or rejects each one. (For example, recognition in the Official RODEOHOUSTON Souvenir Program carries a Dec. 15 deadline.) The panel can also show optional instructional content, which displays in orange font, depending on how the site is configured.

### Additional information panel

For the Souvenir Program, the donor fills in two required fields:

- "I wish to remain anonymous" (required, YES / NO selection).
- "Listing in Souvenir Program" (required, free-form text, 25-character limit). The on-screen helper text notes this applies if your donation is 300 dollars or more, to enter N/A if not applicable, and that the space is limited to 25 characters including letters, punctuation, and spaces, with no phone numbers.

### Payment panel

The Payment panel is always shown and at a minimum allows payment by credit card (with Payment Type, Card Type, Card Number, Name On Card, and Expiration fields). Depending on how the site is configured, other payment options can appear:

- To Be Invoiced (Directors): a Director must provide their customer number as validation.
- To Be Invoiced (limited by dollar amount): if the order exceeds a set dollar amount, the To Be Invoiced option becomes available.
- Send in Payment: this option always requires a customer to be entered for the order.
- eCheck: if the site allows electronic check payments, this can be turned on. It always requires a customer to be entered for the order.

If Invoice, Send in Payment, and eCheck are all allowed, the Payment Type dropdown shows more options.

### Email confirmation panel

The Email Confirmation panel is always shown. It displays the donor's email address by default if a customer is selected. That address can be overwritten with a different one if needed. Whatever email address is entered here will receive the emailed confirmation.

### Confirmation page and emailed confirmation

After the order is submitted, a confirmation page appears showing the order details for all orders. The emailed confirmation includes the same content and goes to the email address entered in the Email Confirmation panel. If a salesperson was selected for the donation, that salesperson is copied on the emailed confirmation, so they know a donation was made.

## FAQ

**When are donations due?**
Donations are due January 30, 2027 and are billed by February 1, 2027.

**How do I get my personal donation link?**
Sign in at www.rodeospc.com, scroll to the Shareable Donation Link widget, and click Copy. The link is now on your clipboard. Paste it wherever you want (email, text, a document).

**How does the link make sure I get credit for the donation?**
Your link carries your sales code (`&salescode=XXXXXXXX`). When the donor clicks it, the donation site opens with your name already selected as the Volunteer, so the gift is credited to you. If a salesperson was selected, you are also copied on the donor's emailed confirmation.

**I am a Rookie and cannot log in. Why?**
If you have not been added to the committee roster yet, you cannot log in to rodeospc.com. Contact your Team Captain.

**As a committee member, where do I actually submit the paper contract?**
Through the SPC online order form. The paper form only collects the information. No forms are accepted anywhere else.

**What is the minimum donation to get my name listed in the Souvenir Program?**
$10,000 for the printed program listing on the paper contract. The listing is limited to 25 characters (letters, punctuation, and spaces), with no phone numbers. If you leave the listing line blank, recognition defaults to the Company or Individual Name on the form. On the online site, the "Listing in Souvenir Program" field applies for donations of $300 or more.

**Do I have to do anything to get my benefits, or are they automatic?**
You have to choose them. The donor must reject or accept each applicable benefit, and no benefit is assumed or given unless it is checked Accepted. Payment also has to be received before any benefits can be distributed. A donor email address is required because benefit notifications and updates go there.

**Who do I contact about donating more than $20,000?**
The HLSR Donor Relations Office at 832.667.1124. They determine the benefits offered above the $20,000 Scholarship Donor level.

**What payment methods can a donor use online?**
Credit card is always available. Depending on the site setup, the donor may also see To Be Invoiced (for Directors or for orders over a set dollar amount), Send in Payment, or eCheck. Send in Payment and eCheck always require a customer to be entered.
