# 2026 Souvenir Program frame order form

**Quick answer:** Use this form to order framed copies of a Souvenir Program ad. A 5x7 frame is $22 cash or check ($23 credit or debit) and an 11x17 frame is $29 cash or check ($30 credit or debit). You can also order online at squareup.com/store/rodeoframes. Questions go to David at 832-236-5601 or Joy at 281-381-8396.

## What this is

A one-page order form for purchasing printed and framed copies of an advertisement that ran in the RODEOHOUSTON Souvenir Program. A member fills in their contact details, lists each ad they want framed (by page number and advertiser name), picks the size and quantity, and pays. The form says to please print, and the year on it is 2026.

## Who to fill out and how to identify the ad

The top of the form collects:

- Name
- Phone
- Address
- Team Number
- Date
- City, St, Zip
- Email

Under "Ad Copy page # and ad name," the form asks you to be specific in describing your ad. There's room to list up to four ads, each with a page number and the advertiser name:

- Page # and Advertiser (line 1)
- Page # and Advertiser (line 2)
- Page # and Advertiser (line 3)
- Page # and Advertiser (line 4)

## Sizes and prices

There are two frame sizes. The cash or check price is lower than the credit or debit price.

| Size | Cash/Check | Credit/Debit |
|---|---|---|
| 5X7 | 22.00 | 23.00 |
| 11 X 17 | 29.00 | 30.00 |

You write in a quantity for each size, multiply by the per-frame price, and put the result in the Total column. The bottom of the pricing block has a final Total line for the whole order.

## How to pay

Mark how you paid: Cash, Credit, or Check. If you pay by check, write the check number on the form and make the check payable to David Hale.

To order online instead, go to https://squareup.com/store/rodeoframes. Put the advertiser name and page number in the "Add additional note to merchant" box on the checkout or payment screen.

## Signature and receipt

The form has a STOP line that reads "DO NOT CONTINUE PAST THIS LINE." Below it is the receipt section. Your signature there indicates receipt of all frames ordered above. There's a line for Signature and a line for Date Received.

## FAQ

**How much does a frame cost?**
A 5x7 is $22 if you pay by cash or check, or $23 by credit or debit. An 11x17 is $29 by cash or check, or $30 by credit or debit.

**Can I order frames online?**
Yes. Go to squareup.com/store/rodeoframes and put the advertiser name and page number in the "Add additional note to merchant" box on the checkout or payment screen.

**Who do I make the check out to?**
David Hale. Write the check number on the form too.

**Who do I call with questions about a frame order?**
David at 832-236-5601 or Joy at 281-381-8396. You can also email joymaddox5@gmail.com.

**How many ads can I list on one form?**
Up to four, each by page number and advertiser name. Be specific when you describe the ad.

**What's the signature line at the bottom for?**
It confirms you received all the frames you ordered. Don't sign until you've received them. There's also a Date Received line.
