# Volunteer Handbook: Officers and executive committee

**Quick answer:** This section lists HLSR's officers. Chris Boleman is President and CEO. Below him are the Lifetime Vice Presidents, each responsible for a group of committees. The Souvenir Program Committee sits under Lifetime Vice President Stan Horton, alongside Directions and Assistance, Judging Contest, Speakers, and Special Attractions.

## What this is

The Officers section of the 2026 Volunteer Handbook. It identifies the President and CEO and the Lifetime Vice Presidents who oversee the Show's committees. Each Lifetime Vice President is paired with the slate of committees in their area of responsibility. If you want to know which officer your committee reports up to, this is the page. The lists below reproduce every officer name and every committee assignment shown in the handbook.

## President and CEO

Chris Boleman is President and CEO.

## Who oversees the Souvenir Program Committee

The Souvenir Program Committee is one of the committees under Lifetime Vice President Stan Horton. The others in his portfolio are Directions and Assistance, Judging Contest, Speakers, and Special Attractions.

## FAQ

**Who oversees the Souvenir Program Committee?**
Lifetime Vice President Stan Horton. The Souvenir Program is one of his committees, along with Directions and Assistance, Judging Contest, Speakers, and Special Attractions.

**Who is the President and CEO?**
Chris Boleman.

**What is a Lifetime Vice President?**
An officer of the Show who oversees a group of committees. The handbook lists each Lifetime Vice President with the committees in their area of responsibility.

**Which Lifetime Vice President handles the wine committees?**
Wine work is split across a few officers. Mark Kelly oversees Wine Garden, Amy Pinkham Miller oversees Wine Events and Wine Sales, and David Smith oversees Wine Competition and Winery Relations.

**Which officer covers membership?**
Stacy Anderson. Her portfolio is Communications and Special Services, Magazine, Membership, Special Children's, and Trailblazer.

**How do I find which officer my committee reports to?**
Look up your committee in the table above. Each row names the Lifetime Vice President and lists the committees in their portfolio.
