# Volunteer Handbook: Committee chairmen

**Quick answer:** This is the committee directory from the 2026 Volunteer Handbook. Each Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo committee has a short description of its work and four leadership contacts: a Vice President (the officer over it), a Chairman (the volunteer lead, shown with the year of their term and a phone number), a Management Coordinator, and a Staff Coordinator. Two committees, Corral Club and Horse Show, also list many sub-committee chairmen. Most Management and Staff Coordinator numbers are HLSR staff lines in the 832.667.1xxx range.

## What this is

The committees section lists the Show's standing volunteer committees alphabetically, with the people who lead each one. The structure of each entry is the same: committee name, the chairman's full name, a paragraph describing the committee's purpose, then the four roles. Corral Club and Horse Show are larger umbrella committees, so after their lead entry they show a grid of sub-committee or division chairmen, each with a term year and phone number.

A few phone numbers in the source are cut off mid-digit by the original document. Those are noted as partial below and the digits are reproduced exactly as printed.

## FAQ

**How is each committee entry organized?**
By committee name and chairman, then a short paragraph on what the committee does, then four roles: Vice President, Chairman (with term year and phone), Management Coordinator, and Staff Coordinator.

**What does the year next to a chairman mean?**
It is the year of their current term as chairman, for example 1st Year, 2nd Year, or 3rd Year. One entry shows 4th Year.

**Which committees have sub-committees?**
Corral Club and Horse Show. Each lists a roster of division or sub-committee chairmen with their own term years and phone numbers, in addition to the lead committee's Vice President and coordinators.

**Why do some committees list a Co-Chairman instead of a Chairman?**
Area Go Texan - East and Area Go Texan - West are each led by a Co-Chairman in this edition.

**Why are a few phone numbers incomplete?**
A handful of Management and Staff Coordinator numbers were cut off in the source document. Those are reproduced exactly as printed and flagged as partial. The full numbers are HLSR staff lines, generally in the 832.667.1xxx range.

**Who do I contact for my committee?**
Use the Chairman for volunteer-side questions and the Management or Staff Coordinator for staff support. The Vice President is the officer over the committee.
