# Volunteer Handbook: Show staff

**Quick answer:** This is the Show staff directory from the 2026 Volunteer Handbook. It lists the paid Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo (HLSR) staff (not the volunteers), with each person's name and job title, grouped by department under the chief officer who leads that area. Departments include the Executive Office, Legal, Finance, Marketing, Mission, Revenue, Operations, Strategic Planning, Technology, and Volunteer & Human Resources.

## What this is

The "Show Staff" section of the handbook is a photo directory of HLSR's employed staff. Each entry is a card with a headshot, the person's name, and their title. The directory is organized by department, and the departments are grouped under the executive officer responsible for them (for example, the Finance department sits under the Chief Financial Officer, and the Marketing departments sit under the Chief Marketing Officer).

This is staff, not volunteers. Volunteers serve on the roughly 105 committees. The people below are the year-round paid employees who run the Show. The tables below capture every entry in the section. A few job titles carry small spelling slips in the source (for example "CONTOLLER", "ACCOUNTS PAYBLE", "STUDIO OPERTAIONS", "NET WORK ADMINISTRATOR", "SOFT WARE DEVELOPER"). These are reproduced as printed.

## FAQ

**Is this list the volunteers or the paid staff?**
This is the paid HLSR staff. Volunteers serve on the committees. The people here are the year-round employees who run the Show.

**How is the directory organized?**
By department, with departments grouped under the executive officer who leads them. The chief officers are Chris Boleman (President & CEO), Katie Grahmann (CFO), Lisa Gagnon (CMO), Julie Bass (Chief Mission Officer), Amber Burda (Chief Revenue Officer), Kyle Olsen (Chief Show Operations Officer), Peter McStravick (Chief Strategic Planning Officer), Steven Gumerman (CTO), and Mel Underhill (Chief Volunteer & Human Resources Officer).

**Who leads my committee or the Souvenir Program area?**
The Show staff directory lists employees by department, not by committee assignment. For a committee's staff liaison, check the committee's own page or the Membership department (Katie Luckett, Director, Membership).

**Who do I contact about marketing or graphics support for a committee?**
Marketing has a Coordinator for Marketing Committee Support (Bri Norton) and two Senior Graphic Designers (Chelsea Riddle and Sandra Lara), under Director of Marketing Muffy King and CMO Lisa Gagnon.

**Some titles have spelling errors. Is that intentional?**
Those are reproduced exactly as printed in the handbook. Examples include "Contoller", "Accounts Payble", "Studio Opertaions", "Network Administrator" (printed "NET WORK"), and "Software Developer" (printed "SOFT WARE").
