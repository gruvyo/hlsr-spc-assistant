# Using the SPC Dashboard (login, RSVP, team change requests)

**Quick answer:** The SPC Dashboard at https://dashboard.rodeospc.com is where the committee shares tools, forms, resources, and communication through the season. Log in regularly. New members RSVP to meetings through the Event Schedule widget, and if you cannot create a login yet it is usually because the roster has not been updated. Submit a team change request at https://dashboard.rodeospc.com/team_change_request.php.

## What this is

The SPC Dashboard at https://dashboard.rodeospc.com is the committee's online home for the season. It is where the committee shares tools, forms, resources, and communication. Key resources like the RSVP link, artwork guidelines, and submission forms all live there, so get in the habit of logging in regularly.

## Logging in as a new member (rookies)

Account creation checks that you are on the committee roster before it lets you finish. If you are a new member and cannot create a login yet, your roster data may not be updated. Wait a few days and try again while leadership keeps the roster current. If you still cannot get in, email a member of leadership so they can resolve your access.

## RSVPing to a meeting

RSVP through the Event Schedule widget on the Dashboard. RSVPs open one week before each event, so if the RSVP button is not there yet, check back closer to the meeting. Complete it as soon as it opens, since your response helps leadership plan the meeting.

## Submitting a team change request

Need to move to a different team? Submit a request at https://dashboard.rodeospc.com/team_change_request.php. The admin team will review it, and you will receive email updates as your request progresses.
