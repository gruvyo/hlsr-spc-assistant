# 2025 Souvenir Program Frame Order Form

**Quick answer:** Use this form to order framed copies of an ad that ran in the Souvenir Program. A 5x7 frame is $22 by cash or check ($23 by credit or debit), and an 11x17 frame is $29 by cash or check ($30 by credit or debit). Fill in the advertiser and page number, pick a size and quantity, and pay David Hale (check made payable to David Hale) or order online at the Square store. Questions go to David at 832-236-5601 or Joy at 281-381-8396.

## What this is

A one-page order form (titled "Souvenir Program Frame Order," marked "PLEASE PRINT," for 2025) that members use to order framed prints of ads that appeared in the RODEOHOUSTON Souvenir Program. A member fills in their own contact details, identifies the specific ad or ads by page number and advertiser name, chooses a frame size and quantity, and pays. The bottom of the form has a signature line that a member signs when they receive the finished frames.

## Member and contact fields

The top of the form collects the ordering member's information:

- Name
- Phone
- Address
- City, State (St), Zip
- Team Number
- Date
- Email

## Identifying the ad to be framed

Under the heading "Ad Copy page # and ad name," the form asks members to be specific in describing the ad. There are four lines, each with two fields:

- Page #
- Advertiser

Fill in the page number where the ad ran and the advertiser name for each frame you want. Be specific so the right ad gets framed.

## Sizes and pricing

Two frame sizes are offered. Prices depend on how you pay, so cash or check is a dollar cheaper per frame than credit or debit.

| Size | Cash or check price | Credit or debit price |
|---|---|---|
| 5x7 | 22.00 | 23.00 |
| 11x17 | 29.00 | 30.00 |

On the form you enter a quantity (Qty) for each size, multiply by the unit price, and write a per-line total. There is a Total line at the bottom for the sum of all frames ordered.

## How to pay

There are two ways to pay.

**In person, by cash, credit, or check.** The form has checkboxes for Cash, Credit, and Check, plus a field for the check number. Make checks payable to David Hale.

**Online, through Square.** Order online at https://squareup.com/store/rodeoframes. On the checkout or payment screen, put the advertiser name and page number in the "Add additional note to merchant" box so your order can be matched to the right ad.

## Questions and contacts

Call David at 832-236-5601 or Joy at 281-381-8396. Email Joy at joymaddox5@gmail.com.

## Receipt and signature

The form has a stop notice near the bottom that reads "STOP" and "DO NOT CONTINUE PAST THIS LINE." Everything below that line is filled in later, not at the time of ordering. There is a signature line and a "Date Received" line, and the form states that a member's signature indicates receipt of all frames ordered above. In other words, you sign only when you actually pick up the finished frames.

## FAQ

**How much does a frame cost?**
A 5x7 is $22 if you pay by cash or check, or $23 by credit or debit. An 11x17 is $29 by cash or check, or $30 by credit or debit.

**Can I order online?**
Yes. Go to https://squareup.com/store/rodeoframes and put the advertiser name and page number in the "Add additional note to merchant" box at checkout.

**Who do I make the check out to?**
David Hale. Write the check number on the form in the space provided.

**Who do I call with questions about a frame order?**
David at 832-236-5601 or Joy at 281-381-8396. You can also email Joy at joymaddox5@gmail.com.

**What information do I need to give for each ad?**
The page number where the ad ran and the advertiser name. Be specific so the correct ad is framed. You can list up to four ads on the form.

**When do I sign the form?**
You sign at the bottom only when you receive the frames. The signature confirms you got all the frames you ordered. Do not fill in anything past the "STOP" line until then.
